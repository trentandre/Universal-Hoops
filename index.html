Who We Are

MORE THAN JUST A PLACE TO TRAIN

Welcome to Universal Hoops — where passion meets purpose and real development happens. Every session is designed with intent, targeting the key areas that impact the game most — footwork, shooting, decision-making, and the ability to create and react under pressure.

Training isn't random or repetitive for the sake of it. It's structured, purposeful, and built around real in-game situations so players can perform with confidence when it matters. Players are challenged to think, move, and compete at a higher level.

MEET YOUR COACHES

Founder & Head Coach

TRENT

Founder and owner of Universal Hoops with 13 years of playing experience. Currently playing NBL1 for Casey, Trent built Universal Hoops to give athletes access to the same standard of development he's experienced at the highest local level. He works with athletes of all ages and skill levels — and has experience with NBA Basketball Schools alongside junior and professional athletes.

NBL1 Player 13 Yrs Experience NBA Schools

Coach

JACK

A versatile player with 9 years of competitive experience, currently competing in Big V Division 2 Men's for Sherbrooke Suns. Jack played Big V Division 1 at just 18, developed through Templestowe College's Basketball Academy under Olympic coach Ian Stacker, and competed in the University Basketball League with La Trobe against NBL1-level talent. Since joining Universal Hoops in 2025, Jack delivers results-driven coaching tailored to each athlete — combining experience, attention to detail, and genuine care.
